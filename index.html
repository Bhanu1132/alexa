About Alexa Chain
AlexaChain is an EVM-compatible blockchain that aims to complement the $Alexa cryptocurrency. As a proof-of-stake blockchain, AlexaChain seeks to bring scalability, security, robustness, and utility to $Alexa.
Alexachain envisions that interoperable blockchain technology makes the world economy more resilient through decentralization, more accountability through transparency, and more efficiency through programmable value. The future economy consists of more than one million interconnected blockchains that exchange real-world and scarce digital assets.
